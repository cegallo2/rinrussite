About Us

Residue Interaction Network-based ResidUe Selector (RINRUS), is a software toolkit that guides selection of atoms in a protein structure to be included in the QM-cluster, QM/MM, or ONIOM model.


RINRUS is written in Python and is available on the GitHub page of Dr. Qianyi Cheng: https://github.com/MiloCheng17/RINRUS

Please contact Prof. Nathan DeYonker (ndyonker@memphis.edu) for more details.
